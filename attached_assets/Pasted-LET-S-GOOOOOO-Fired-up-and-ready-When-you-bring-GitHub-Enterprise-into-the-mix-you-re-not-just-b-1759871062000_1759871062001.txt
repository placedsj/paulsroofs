### \#\# 🚀 Step 2: The Game Plan

1.  **Create a new `HTML, CSS, JS` Repl.**
2.  **Create the `images` folder.** Upload all the necessary images to it.
3.  **Copy & Paste:** Paste the code blocks above into their corresponding `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js` files in Replit. Delete the default content first.
4.  **Set Your Secret:** Click the padlock icon (🔒), create a new secret with the key `GEMINI_API_KEY`, and paste your actual Google API key as the value.
5.  **Connect to GitHub:** In the left sidebar, go to the "Git" tab and connect it to your GitHub Enterprise account. You can now commit these clean, separated files and manage your project like a pro.
6.  **LAUNCH:** Hit the **"Run"** button and watch it all come to life.

You now have a clean, manageable, and scalable codebase ready for whatever automation and CI/CD pipelines you want to throw at it. Let's build\!
